Chance Engagement

Setup

In the center of playmat (Range 4,5 of each border), place a small ship token without its base. It represents a an automatic Turret. Each player may take control of the turret by performing a Scenario Action at range 0-1 of it.
SCENARIO ACTION: if you are at range 0-1 of the central turret, you gain control of it (place a token). You can rotate it 90° or 180° (if you already control it you can rotate it).
It attacks like a ship at Initiative 0 : 3 attack dice range 1-3 Special Attack. The defender may also cancel #eye# results with #evd# results (#crit# results can be canceled before #eye# results). If he doesn't, he receives 1 deplete token per each remaining #eye# results.

Objective

Destroy your opponent ships. When you destroy half a ship, you gain scenario points equal to half the value of the ship (rounded up). The game ends at the end of the clock, or if a player has no more ships on the battlefield.

Struggle Bonus

The Loser gains a Victory Point if he has at least 1 remaining ship on the battlefield, and the difference between the winner and the losers's scenario points is 5 Points or less.

Domination Bonus

The winner gains a Victory point if he scored 30 Points or more and the losers has won less than 15 scenario points

Bounty Hunting

2 bounty ship tokens are placed (HULL8 Agility 2). They shoot every ship at range 3 at initiative 7 (Range 3 1 Dice; Range 2 : 2 dice, Range 1 : 3 dice) One player controls one, the other player controls the other one (There's a dial for each ship). When a player destroys one bounty ship, he gains 5 points. When a player land a critical hit on a bounty ship, he gains 1 point. When you destroy an enemy ship, if the bounty ship has been destroyed, you score as many scenario points as the value of the destroyed ship ; if the opponent bounty ship is still on the board, you score only half value.

Struggle Bonus

the Loser gains a Victory Point if he has at least 1 remaining ship on the battlefield, and the difference between the winner and the losers's scenario points is 5 Points or less.

Domination Bonus

Your Bounty ship has not been destroyed and you have destroyed the opponent's bounty ship

Escort Mission

Setup

Each player controls a small ship token (or a senator shuttle) (HULL 8 Agility 2). They place it in their starting zone. Then each player place a crate. The ship token must fly at range 0/1 of the allied crate and do a salvage action. Then he must fly out of the playing area to flee. If a ship token succeeds fleeing with the crate, the player gains 15 points. If the opponent destroys the opponents' token ship, he gains 5 points.
Any ship can do an Escort Action : at range 0/1 of the token ship. The ship Token gains a focus token. When the ship token is being hit, you can transfer 1 damage to a ship which has done an escort action. When a regular ship is destroyed, the opponent gains scenario points equal to the half value of the ship.
Maneuvers available for the ship token : Straight : 1, 2, 3, 4 : Bank : 1, 2, 3 ; Turn : 2. No K Turn.

Struggle Bonus

The Loser gains a Victory Point if he has at least 1 remaining ship on the battlefield, and the difference between the winner and the losers's scenario points is 5 Points or less.

Domination Bonus

You have destroyed tho opponent's ship token and your ship token has fled the battlefield with the crate

Salvage Mission

Same than in 2.5, except that only half points are scored when ships are destroyed.

Struggle Bonus

Domination Bonus

The winner gains a Victory point if he scored 30 Points or more and the losers has won less than 15 scenario points

Assault and Defense

Assymetrical scenario. The Attack player and the defense player are chosen randomly. The defense player place all 4 satellites wherever he wants, but they must not overlap with obstacles, and they have to be beyond range 3 of each other.
The attack player must destroy 4 Satellites (3Hull 1Agility).
When a Satellite is destroyed, he gains 5 scenario points and the defense player loses 2 scenario points. The attack player scores half value for any enemy ship destroyed.
The defense player scores full value for any enemy ship destroyed. During System Phase, the defense player may relocate 1 of the satellites. To relocate, 1 satellite uses a speed 3 template. Then all satellites may perform a jam action (Range 0-1 except)

Struggle Bonus

The Loser gains a Victory Point if he has at least 1 remaining ship on the battlefield, and the difference between the winner and the losers's scenario points is 5 Points or less.

Domination Bonus

The winner gains a Victory point if :
- ATTACK : All objectives have been destroyed and DEFENSE player has less than 10 scenario points
- DEFENSE : All enemy ships have been destroyed and ATTACK player has less than 15 scenario points
