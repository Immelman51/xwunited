Chance Engagement

Setup

In the center of playmat (Range 4,5 of each border), place a small ship token without its base. It represents an automatic Turret. Each player may take control of the turret by performing a Scenario Action at range 0-1 of it.
SCENARIO ACTION: if you are at range 0-1 of the central turret, you gain control of it (place a token). You can rotate it 90° or 180° (if you already control it you can rotate it).
It attacks like a ship at Initiative 0 : 3 attack dice range 1-3 Special Attack. The defender may also cancel #eye# results with #evd# results (#crit# results can be canceled before #eye# results). If he doesn't, he receives 1 deplete token per each remaining #eye# results.

Objective

Destroy your opponent ships. When you destroy half a ship, you gain scenario points equal to half the value of the ship (rounded up). The game ends at the end of the clock, or if a player has no more ships on the battlefield.

Struggle Bonus

The Loser gains a Tournament Point if he has at least 1 remaining ship on the battlefield, and the difference between the winner and the losers's scenario points is 5 Points or less.

Domination Bonus

The winner gains a Tournament point if he scored 30 Points or more and the losers has won less than 15 scenario points

Dead or Alive

Setup

In addition to its ships, starting player places a bounty ship token (small ship token) at initiative 7 in the middle of the battlefield.
Its stats are (HULL 12 Agility 2). It shoots EVERY ship within range 3 at initiative 7 : roll 3 attack dice : ships at range 3 defend first. Then upgrade 1 attack dice (eye first, else hit, else blank). Ships at range 2 defend. Then upgrade again a die (eye first, else hit, else blank). Ships at range 1 defend.
Each player may bring any dials to control the Bounty Ship. During planning phase, Starting player chooses 1 of the dials brought by both players, then he picks randomly a maneuver. Then the other player randomizes also the maneuver. The Bounty Ship cannot stress. If there's a doubt on the maneuver indicated on the dial, the starting player chooses 1 of 2 possible maneuvers.

Objective

When the bounty ship is destroyed, each player gains as many scenario points as the number of damage cards he has inflicted him.
SCENARIO ACTION : CAPTURE : (At range 0-1 of the bounty ship) Place 1 Capture Token on the Bounty ship. When a player has 3 more Capture Token than his opponnent, he is "captured" by this player who wins 10 scenario points.
When you destroy an enemy ship, if the bounty ship has been destroyed or captured, you score as many scenario points as the value of the destroyed ship ; if the opponent bounty ship is still on the board, you score only half value.

Struggle Bonus

the Loser gains a Tournament Point if he has at least 1 remaining ship on the battlefield, and the difference between the winner and the losers's scenario points is 5 Points or less.

Domination Bonus

Your Bounty ship has not been destroyed and you have destroyed the opponent's bounty ship

Escort Mission

Setup

Each player controls a small ship token (or a senator shuttle) (HULL 8 Agility 2). They place it in their starting zone at initiative 7. Then each player place a crate.
Any ship can do an Escort Action : at range 0/1 of the token ship. The ship Token gains a focus token. When the ship token is being hit, you can transfer 1 damage to a ship which has done an escort action. When a regular ship is destroyed, the opponent gains scenario points equal to the half value of the ship.
Maneuvers available for the ship token : Straight : 1, 2, 3, 4 : Bank : 1, 2, 3 ; Turn : 2. No K Turn.

Objective

The ship token must fly at range 0/1 of the allied crate and do a salvage action. Then he must fly out of the playing area to flee. If a ship token succeeds fleeing with the crate, the player gains 15 points. If the opponent destroys the opponents' token ship, he gains 5 points.

Struggle Bonus

The Loser gains a Tournament Point if he has at least 1 remaining ship on the battlefield, and the difference between the winner and the losers's scenario points is 5 Points or less.

Domination Bonus

You have destroyed tho opponent's ship token and your ship token has fled the battlefield with the crate

Salvage Mission

Setup

At the start of the Place Obstacles step of Setup, place one supply cache in the center of the play area (centered at range 4.5 of all board edges). Then, starting with the first player, players take turns placing the remaining 2 supply caches at range 3 of the center supply cache marker and beyond range 2 of each other supply cache. The supply cache placed by each player must be placed at range 2–4 of their board edge. The Obstacles cannot be placed overlapping any supply cache.
Scenario Action: [Tow]
Tow: When a ship performs a tow action, choose a supply cache at range 0–1. Remove the chosen supply cache from the play area and place it on the ship’s card. A ship can have only one supply cache on its card. Then, place one of your player markers next to the ship’s miniature to show that it is towing a supply cache. If there is no supply cache in range, the action fails. After a ship performs a tow action, it removes any cloak tokens it has.
Before revealing the dial of a ship carrying a cache, its owner may jettison its cache by placing the cache marker within range 1 of the ship. While a ship is towing a supply cache, if it performs a #BR# or #BO# or #CK# or #SLAM# action, it gains a deplete token and a strain token.

Objective

At the start of the End Phase, earn 1 scenario point for each supply cache on a friendly ship’s card.except that only half points are scored when ships are destroyed. The game ends at the end of the clock or when a player has no more ships on the battlefield.

Struggle Bonus

The Loser gains a Tournament Point if he has at least 1 remaining ship on the battlefield, and the difference between the winner and the losers's scenario points is 5 Points or less.

Domination Bonus

The winner gains a Tournament point if he scored 30 Points or more and the losers has won less than 20 scenario points

Assault and Defense

Setup

Assymetrical scenario. The Attack player and the defense player are chosen randomly. The defense player place all 4 satellites wherever he wants, but they must not overlap with obstacles, and they have to be beyond range 3 of each other.
The attack player must destroy 4 Satellites (3Hull 1Agility).
During System Phase, the defense player may relocate 1 of the satellites. To relocate, 1 satellite uses a speed 3 template. Then all satellites may perform a jam action (Range 0-1 except)

Objective

When a Satellite is destroyed, he gains 5 scenario points and the defense player loses 2 scenario points. The attack player scores half value for any enemy ship destroyed.
The defense player scores full value for any enemy ship destroyed.
The game ends at the end of the clock, or if a player has no more ships on the board.

Struggle Bonus

The Loser gains a Tournament Point if he has at least 1 remaining ship on the battlefield, and the difference between the winner and the losers's scenario points is 5 Points or less.

Domination Bonus

The winner gains a Tournament point if :
- ATTACK : All objectives have been destroyed and DEFENSE player has less than 10 scenario points
- DEFENSE : All enemy ships have been destroyed and ATTACK player has less than 15 scenario points
